My lab houlab.org opened shop in July 2022 at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory where I am currently an Assistant Professor. We are actively recuiting Research Technicians, Students, and Postdocs. If you are interested in working with us, please contact me directly via hou [at] cshl.edu with your CV/resume, cover letter, and contact information for three references.

Previously, I was a visiting research scientist in neuromotor interfaces at the CTRL-labs team at Reality Labs. I was a postdoc research scientist and Helen Hay Whitney Fellow at the Zuckerman Mind Brain Behavior Institute at Columbia University, working with Nate Sawtell and Larry Abbott. Previously, I obtained my B.Sc. in Physics and Biology from MIT, was a grad student with Bernardo Sabatini at Harvard University, and a Grass Fellow at Marine Biological Laboratory.
